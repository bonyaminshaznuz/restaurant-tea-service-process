# Detailed Process Description

This document explains the tea service process in a restaurant.

## Step 1 – Customer Arrival
The customer enters the restaurant and sits at a table.

## Step 2 – Calling the Waiter
The customer calls the waiter to place an order.

## Step 3 – Taking the Order
The waiter records the tea order.

## Step 4 – Sending Order to Chef
The waiter communicates the order to the chef.

## Step 5 – Preparing Tea
The chef prepares the tea.

## Step 6 – Serving the Tea
The waiter brings the prepared tea to the customer.

## Step 7 – Consumption
The customer drinks the tea.

## Step 8 – Billing
The waiter generates the bill and brings it to the customer.

## Step 9 – Payment
The customer pays the bill.

## Step 10 – Process End
The service process is completed.
