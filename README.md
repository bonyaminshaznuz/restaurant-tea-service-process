# Restaurant Tea Service Process

This repository contains a **process flow diagram** representing how tea is ordered, prepared, served, and billed in a restaurant environment.

The diagram models the interaction between **Customer, Waiter, Chef, and Manager**.

It demonstrates a simple **end-to-end service workflow** used in restaurant operations.

---

## Process Overview

The workflow begins when a **customer sits and calls the waiter** and ends when the **bill is paid**.

### Main Steps

1. Customer sits and calls the waiter
2. Waiter takes the tea order
3. Waiter sends the order to the chef
4. Chef prepares the tea
5. Waiter brings the tea to the customer
6. Customer drinks the tea
7. Waiter generates the bill
8. Bill is brought to the customer
9. Customer pays the bill
10. Process ends

---

## Actors in the Process

| Actor | Responsibility |
|-----|-----|
| Customer | Orders tea, drinks tea, pays bill |
| Waiter | Takes order, delivers tea, prepares bill |
| Chef | Prepares tea |
| Manager / Billing | Verifies and processes bill |
