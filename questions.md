# Process Analysis Questions

## Basic Questions

1. What is the starting point of the process?
2. Who are the main actors in this process?
3. What are the responsibilities of the waiter?
4. What role does the chef play in the workflow?
5. When is the bill generated?

## Process Improvement Questions

1. How can this process be optimized to reduce waiting time?
2. What happens if the tea preparation is delayed?
3. How could technology improve this workflow?
4. How can errors in billing be prevented?
5. How could digital ordering improve efficiency?

## Risk Analysis

Potential risks in the process:

- Waiter forgets to send order
- Chef delays preparation
- Bill calculation errors
- Customer waits too long

Possible improvements:

- Digital order system
- Kitchen display system
- Automated billing
